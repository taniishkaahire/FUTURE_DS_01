# Business Sales Performance Analysis

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The objective is to generate business insights and actionable recommendations to improve overall sales performance.

##  Objectives
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate country-wise sales performance
- Provide strategic business recommendations

##  Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

##  Key Analysis Performed
1. Data Cleaning (removed returns and invalid transactions)
2. Revenue Calculation (Quantity × Unit Price)
3. Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
4. Top 10 Products by Revenue
5. Top 10 Countries by Revenue
6. KPI Calculation (Total Revenue, Orders, Customers)

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong growth in the last quarter of the year.
- Month 11 recorded the highest sales.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing efforts before peak sales months.
- Expand sales strategies in underperforming regions.
